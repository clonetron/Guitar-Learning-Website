Frequently Asked Questions

How much does it cost?

JPGuitar is completely free of charge. What are you waiting for? Start learning now!

Where can I buy a guitar?

You can buy a guitar at many different places. The preffered place to buy a guitar would be at music stores, as the selection for guitars as well as the accessories are abundant.

How come I'm not a master at guitar yet?

JPGuitar is meant to be a beginner's introduction into learning guitar. Although you might have finished our lessons, the journey of learning guitar is far from over. The number one secret to mastering guitar? Practice!
